About

Botany with Qui(Quee) is a blog dedicated to a dear friend that is on a journey of growth and inner peace through her plants. With this blog she will be able to record her progress and experiences. Planting seeds has become one of her passions and this site with it's dimmed-lit and serene vibe will assist her on her path. Thank you for visiting Botany with Qui.
